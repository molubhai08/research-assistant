Core Capabilities

Built for how
researchers think

Every decision the agent makes—whether to answer from memory, retrieve from your project, or search arXiv live—happens in milliseconds, invisibly and accurately.

01
Intelligent Query Routing
LangGraph agent decides in real-time: direct answer, RAG over embeddings, or fresh arXiv search—based on query type and project context.
02
Local TF-IDF RAG
Papers are chunked and indexed locally using a high-speed TF-IDF vector store with per-project scoping for accurate, contextual retrieval.
03
Live arXiv Search
When your project's knowledge isn't enough, the agent fetches, parses, and integrates fresh papers from arXiv on demand—automatically.
04
Persistent Projects
SQLite and Django ORM store your projects, chat histories, and saved research findings. Every session is stateful and project-scoped.
05
Secure Authentication
Standard Django Authentication guards every request through the back end—your research data stays private and project-scoped.
06
Saved Research Findings
Pin key insights from any conversation to your project. Build a living knowledge base that grows with every research session.
